# 📊 Regional Employment Patterns in Tamil Nadu (Excel & Power BI Project)

## 📌 Overview

This project presents an interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes employment data across districts in Tamil Nadu. It focuses on workforce distribution, job card issuance, and participation trends to provide meaningful insights into regional employment patterns.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze registered vs active workers across districts
* Study workforce distribution by social categories
* Compare male and female workforce participation
* Track job card application vs issuance trends
* Identify regional disparities in employment

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI Desktop
* Data Modeling
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Visualization
* Interactive Filters & Slicers

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

* 📍 **District-wise Worker Distribution**
* 👥 **Registered Workers by Social Category**
* 📈 **Active vs Registered Workers Trend**
* 🚻 **Women vs Male Workforce Analysis**
* 📊 **Job Card Application vs Issuance Comparison**
* 🎯 **Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):**

  * Job Card Issue Rate
  * Total Workers
  * Active Workers
  * Women Participation Rate

---

## 📈 Key Metrics

* **Total Workers:** 13 Million
* **Active Workers:** 9 Million
* **Women Participation Rate:** 70%
* **Job Card Issue Rate:** 0.98 (Above target)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Significant variation in workforce distribution across districts
* High female participation (~70%) in employment programs
* Active workers are slightly lower than registered workers, indicating scope for engagement improvement
* Job card issuance rate is close to target, showing efficient processing
* Some districts show higher employment concentration, highlighting regional imbalance
